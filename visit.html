Tube Coffee vs Tube Coffee Plus

Tube Coffee

The classic kiosk style location built for speed and convenience. Walk in, grab your favorite drink, and go, all in under a few minutes. With 35 standard outlets across Phnom Penh and the provinces, these stores focus on takeaway beverages at the lowest prices. Perfect for your morning rush, a quick afternoon pick me up, or anything you need on the move.

Tube Coffee Plus

The upgraded experience, a full size cafe with spacious seating, ambient lighting, and a complete food menu alongside the drinks you already love. Currently 8 locations including the flagship store at Samai Square. Tube Coffee Plus is designed for longer visits. Study with friends, hold casual meetings, or sit down for a proper meal. Same affordable prices, just a bigger space to enjoy them.

Store Locations

Phnom Penh, 37 Stores

Tube Coffee kiosks are on almost every major street and intersection in the capital. From Russian Federation Blvd to Monivong Blvd, you are never far from one.

Provinces, 6 Stores

Tube Coffee has expanded beyond the capital to serve customers in key provincial cities, bringing the same quality and affordable prices nationwide.

Tube Coffee Plus, 8 Locations

The first Tube Coffee Plus opened at Samai Square on January 8, 2021. These larger locations offer a full food menu and spacious seating, perfect for longer visits.

What to Order on Your First Visit

  • Thnol Coffee: Their signature iced coffee. Bold, sweet, and uniquely Khmer.
  • Green Milk Tea: Creamy, refreshing, and a great lighter alternative.
  • Fresh Passion Juice: Made with real fruit. Perfect for hot afternoons.
  • Latte: A reliable classic for when you want something familiar but well made.

Pro tip: If you want a proper sit down experience, visit a Tube Coffee Plus location. They serve food alongside drinks, great for lunch or a study session with friends.
