About Tube Coffee

A homegrown Cambodian coffee brand that started with a dream and grew into a national movement.

The Story Behind the Brand

Tube Coffee was founded in 2017 by three young Cambodian coffee lovers named Oknha Khorn Chhundara, Ms. Sou Monypich, and Oknha Lay Bunnavath. They shared a simple but powerful vision to create a coffee brand that truly represents Khmer people and culture. What started as a single kiosk at Kdan Two in Phnom Penh has grown into a chain of 43 stores across Cambodia, with 37 in the capital and 6 more spread throughout the provinces.

In 2021, they launched Tube Coffee Plus at Samai Square, a larger concept with full food menus and spacious seating areas. Today there are 8 Tube Coffee Plus locations. The brand is registered under Tube Cafe Co., Ltd. and continues to expand rapidly.

Key milestone: From one kiosk in March 2017 to 43 stores in less than a decade. That is growth powered by genuine customer loyalty and a commitment to quality at affordable prices.

What Makes Tube Coffee Different

Unlike international chains that charge premium prices, Tube Coffee keeps its drinks affordable without sacrificing quality. A coffee here costs between $1.50 and $3.00, which is a fraction of what you would pay at most competitors. The menu spans coffee, espresso drinks, fresh juices, milk teas, and smoothies, with Tube Coffee Plus locations also offering a full food menu.

Tube Coffee's Mission

Tube Coffee believes that the coffee industry is one of the leading sectors in Cambodia, and they are determined to set the standard for fast, affordable, and quality beverage service. Their exponential growth proves that the demand for accessible premium drinks has never been higher.
