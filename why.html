Why It Is My Favorite Coffee Spot

Three reasons this local brand earned a permanent spot in my daily routine.

Why Tube Coffee?

100% Khmer Owned

Founded by three young Cambodians in 2017 who dreamed of a coffee brand that truly represents Khmer people. Every cup supports local growth.

Fast and Affordable

Premium quality drinks and food at prices that do not break the bank. Your daily caffeine fix should be accessible to everyone.

43 Stores Across Cambodia

From Phnom Penh to the provinces, Tube Coffee is everywhere you need it, 37 stores in the capital and 6 more across the country.

Why I Like It, My Personal Take

01

I Love That It Is Homegrown

In a country where international coffee chains dominate the market, Tube Coffee proves that a local brand can compete and win. I chose this business because it represents Cambodian entrepreneurship at its best. Three young people turned a dream into 43 stores without any foreign backing. Every time I buy a drink there, I feel like I am supporting something bigger than just a coffee shop. I am supporting a movement that says Khmer brands can stand tall on their own.

02

The Value Is Unbeatable

I am a student, and budget matters. Tube Coffee gives me a quality drink for around $1.50 to $2.00, something most other cafes simply cannot match. But the low price does not mean low quality. Their Thnol Coffee is genuinely one of the best iced coffees I have ever had, and their fresh juices taste like actual fruit, not syrup. The combination of fair pricing and real quality is what keeps me coming back instead of trying somewhere else.

03

It Is Always Within Reach

Living in Phnom Penh, I can walk or ride a short distance and find a Tube Coffee on almost every major street. That kind of accessibility is not an accident. It is the result of deliberate expansion to serve everyday people. Whether I need a morning coffee before class or a cold drink after work, there is always a store nearby. That convenience, combined with consistency across all locations, is something I genuinely appreciate.
